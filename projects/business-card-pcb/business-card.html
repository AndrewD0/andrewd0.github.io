Project Overview:

I designed and built a PCB business card that I can show to recruiters and potential employers. The card contains an NFC chip that can be scanned to access my LinkedIn profile and resume. I designed the PCB using Altium and had it manufactured by a PCB fabrication company. The card also features a custom antenna design to ensure reliable NFC communication.
